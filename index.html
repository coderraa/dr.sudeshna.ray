About Dr. Sudeshna Ray

Advancing the science and art of women's health.

Dr. Sudeshna Ray, MD, FRCOG (London) is an obstetrician and gynaecologist of repute — popular amongst her patients, colleagues and academicians, and practising for the last 25 years. She is a Fellow of the Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists, London, the highest recognition given in the UK for her contribution to advancing the field.

She believes in empowering women with the knowledge to participate in their own decision-making, and is passionate about advanced, less-invasive care — non-surgical options, scarless, robotic and keyhole procedures, and multidisciplinary management when required.

"Our mission is to empower women to prioritise their health, participate in informed decision making and lead happier, healthier lives."
